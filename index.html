A Legacy of Wisdom and Justice

Hajiya Saratu Mama Shafii, born in 1962 in Jos, Plateau State, stands as a testament to the power of intellect, integrity, and unwavering dedication. With roots in Niger State, she has woven a tapestry of professional excellence and personal grace that continues to inspire generations.

Her journey embodies the perfect harmony of legal precision and creative expression, a rare combination that has illuminated both courtrooms and literary circles. Through decades of service to Nigeria's justice system and her award-winning literary contributions, she has established an enduring legacy that transcends professional boundaries.

The Journey of Justice

1962

Birth in Jos

Born in the vibrant city of Jos, Plateau State, with ancestral roots in Niger State.

1985

Called to the Nigerian Bar

After studying Law at Ahmadu Bello University, she was called to the Nigerian Bar, marking the beginning of her distinguished legal career.

1986-1991

Niger State Ministry of Justice

Served as a legal officer, contributing to the administration of justice in Niger State.

1992

Corporate Affairs Commission

Joined the CAC as Senior Litigation Officer, beginning a decades-long commitment to corporate regulation in Nigeria.

1997

Literary Achievement

Published her award-winning novel "Dazzle", showcasing her creative talents alongside her legal career.

2012

Director of Incorporated Trustees

Promoted to Director, heading the Incorporated Trustees Department at CAC.

2015

Nigerian Law School Alumni Merit Award

Recognized by her peers for outstanding contributions to the legal profession.

December 2019

Acting Registrar-General

Appointed Acting Registrar-General of the Corporate Affairs Commission, capping a remarkable career of service.

Literary Legacy

Beyond her legal accomplishments, Hajiya Saratu Mama Shafii demonstrated remarkable creative talent through her award-winning novel "Dazzle". This literary work showcases her ability to weave compelling narratives while exploring complex human experiences.

The novel received the prestigious Delta Fiction Award, highlighting her unique voice in Nigerian literature. Her dual mastery of legal precision and creative expression stands as a testament to the multifaceted nature of her intellect and contributions to Nigerian culture.
